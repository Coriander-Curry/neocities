Projects~

I'm the sort of person that always has a rotation of projects across different interests but then can get quickly overwhelmed but whatever nonsense and shelve the project for a new shiny one. This year (2026) I'm hoping can be the year of completions. Completing all of my half finished or barely started projects as well as wrapping up any new ones that I start. I think I will even assign a number to each project 1->n and then use a random number generator when I'm feeling indecisive or just need a nudge in some direction.

1. Murder in the Alps M-MQAL

Type: Quilt

Started: 1/14/2026

Status: In progress; projected completion 12/2026

"Murder on the Orient Express" is the theme of this Murder Mystery Quilt Along. Each month a chapter of a murder mystery is shared as well as a quilt block. The fabric requirements are given at the beginning of the year but no pictures or diagrams of the finished quilt. This one is a pretty laid back project and due to the nature of the project can't be rushed.

2. Pskov Viking Underdress

Type: Clothing / Historical Clothing

Started: 2025

Status: In progress

I still need to trim and hem the test garment for this so I can wear it around the house. The fit of it feels a bit off but I think that may be due to all the pieces I had to stitch together to get long enough panels. At the time I was trying to make use of every inch of the nice 100% cotton fabric from the vintage yukata as I could but it kind of bit me in the ass. The piecing makes it drape in a stiff way and I should have trimmed down some pieces. Overall it is an excellent test in that I know all the things I have to change before cutting up the $$$ linen.

Note: This is part of an ongoing effort to go back to making my own clothes
