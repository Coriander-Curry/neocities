1. Murder in the Alps M-MQAL

Type: Quilt

Started: 1/14/2026

Status: Ongoing~

"Murder on the Orient Express" is the theme of this Murder Mystery Quilt Along. Each month a chapter of a murder mystery is shared as well as a quilt block. The fabric requirements are given at the beginning of the year but no pictures or diagrams of the finished quilt. This one is a pretty laid back project and due to the nature of the project can't be rushed.
